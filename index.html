What is Pop-it?

Pop it literally translates as "pop it". This is a child's game, the process of which can be compared to the bursting of an air-bubble wrapping film. But unlike film, pop its can be pressed with the characteristic sensation and sound "infinitely": dozens of silicone bubbles are simply squeezed out in the other direction.

Rules of the game

Players take turns and click on the pimples. It is necessary to finish the series that you started. You can click on a maximum of 3 pimples at the same time (they must be nearby), you can't press at once. Several pimples if they are not right next to each other" The task is to make the opponent go last. Whoever went last, lost!
